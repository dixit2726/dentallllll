What Does Smile Designing Include?

We combine multiple advanced procedures to deliver a complete transformation.

Teeth Whitening

Professional-grade whitening that removes years of stains from coffee, tea, and age, brightening your smile by several shades in a single session.

Dental Veneers

Ultra-thin, custom-made porcelain shells bonded to the front of teeth to correct shape, size, color, and minor misalignments permanently.

Gum Contouring

Composite Bonding

Using tooth-colored resin to repair minor chips, close small gaps, or reshape teeth without removing significant enamel.

Orthodontic Synergy

Role of Invisalign

One of the most important components of Smile Designing is correcting misaligned teeth. This is where Invisalign plays a crucial role. Invisalign is a modern orthodontic solution that uses clear, removable aligners to straighten teeth gradually.

Unlike traditional braces, Invisalign aligners are nearly invisible and provide a comfortable treatment experience. When integrated into a smile design plan, they provide the perfectly aligned foundation needed for other cosmetic enhancements like whitening or veneers.

  • Invisible Appearance: Straighten teeth without anyone noticing.
  • Comfortable: No sharp wires or metal brackets.
  • Removable: Maintain perfect hygiene throughout treatment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Smile Designing?

Smile Designing is a cosmetic dental procedure that improves the appearance of your smile using multiple treatments such as veneers, whitening, and contouring.

Is Smile Designing painful?

Most procedures involved in smile designing are minimally invasive and painless. We use local anesthesia or sedation for maximum comfort when needed.

How long does it take?

Depending on the treatments selected, it can take anywhere from a few days (for whitening and bonding) to a few weeks (for custom veneers).

Is Invisalign part of Smile Designing?

Yes, Invisalign is often used as the first step in Smile Designing to properly align the teeth before cosmetic enhancements are applied.

Can I preview my smile?

Yes! Using our Digital Smile Design technology, we can create a simulation of your results before we even start the treatment.

How long do results last?

With proper oral hygiene and regular dental visits, results from veneers and bonding can last 10 to 15 years or more.
