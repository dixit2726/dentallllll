Beyond Standard Dentistry

Full Mouth Rehabilitation (also known as full mouth reconstruction or restoration) is one of the most advanced and comprehensive dental treatments designed to restore the health, function, and aesthetics of your entire mouth. It is not just about fixing one or two teeth—it is a complete transformation of your oral health.

This treatment involves rebuilding or restoring all the teeth in both the upper and lower jaws. It is the gold standard for patients who have multiple complex dental problems such as missing teeth, severe decay, worn-down teeth, gum disease, or bite issues.

If you are searching for the best dental clinic in Dammaiguda, Hyderabad, Telangana, Full Mouth Rehabilitation at Vijaya Dental & Implant Center offers a premium and highly personalized solution for long-term dental wellness.

Life-Changing Benefits

Why Choose Rehabilitation?

Many people ignore dental issues until they become severe. Full Mouth Rehabilitation is recommended when multiple problems affect your oral health simultaneously. It addresses:

  • Functional Restoration: Reclaim the ability to eat any food comfortably.
  • Pain Relief: Eliminate chronic jaw pain, headaches, and tooth sensitivity.
  • Aesthetic Transformation: A natural, radiant smile that boosts your self-confidence.
  • Biological Stability: Stops the cycle of decay and gum disease across the whole mouth.

Bio-Mimetic Materials

At Vijaya Dental, we believe that restorations should be indistinguishable from natural teeth. We use bio-compatible materials like Zirconia and Lithium Disilicate (E-Max) which mimic the translucency and wear-resistance of natural enamel.

These materials are not only beautiful but also incredibly strong, capable of withstanding the heavy biting forces required for a functional, full-mouth restoration.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the entire process take?

Depending on the complexity, Full Mouth Rehabilitation can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months. We often phase the treatment to ensure the jaw muscles and joints adapt comfortably to the new bite.

Is the treatment painful?

No. We use advanced local anesthesia and can provide sedation if needed. Most patients are surprised at how comfortable the process is when handled by experts using modern techniques.

Will it look natural?

Absolutely. Our "Digital Smile Design" process ensures that your new teeth match your facial proportions, skin tone, and age, creating a look that is radiant yet believable.

How long do the results last?

With proper hygiene and regular dental checkups, the results of a Full Mouth Rehabilitation can last for 15–20 years or even a lifetime.

Can I eat normally after the treatment?

Yes! One of the primary goals of this treatment is to restore full chewing function. You will be able to enjoy all your favorite foods again without hesitation.

What is the role of Invisalign in this?

We use Invisalign to align your natural teeth first. This often reduces the amount of tooth preparation needed for crowns and creates a much more stable and long-lasting final result.
