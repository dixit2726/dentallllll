The Process

How Invisalign Works

A simple, precise, and patient-friendly journey to your perfect smile.

1

Consultation

Professional assessment at Vijaya Dental to determine if Invisalign is suitable for your specific dental needs.

2

Digital Scanning

We use advanced 3D scanning technology to create a precise digital map of your teeth — no messy molds required.

3

Custom Aligners

Based on your 3D scan, a series of custom-designed, medical-grade plastic aligners are crafted specifically for you.

4

Wear & Transform

Wear your aligners for 20–22 hours daily, switching to a new set every 1-2 weeks as your teeth gradually move.

The Science Behind Your Transformation

Invisalign isn't just about clear plastic; it's about sophisticated biomechanics. Every aligner in your series is digitally engineered to apply the exact amount of force needed to a specific tooth at the precise time.

This controlled force triggers a biological process called "bone remodeling." As your teeth move, the jawbone around the roots gradually adjusts to support the new, perfectly aligned position, ensuring long-term stability and health.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Invisalign painful?

It is generally comfortable, but slight pressure may be felt when switching to a new set of aligners.

How long does it take?

Treatment usually takes 6 to 18 months, depending on the complexity of the case.

Can I eat with aligners?

No, you should remove your aligners while eating to prevent damage and maintain hygiene.

Are results permanent?

Yes, but retainers are needed after treatment to maintain the final position of your teeth.
